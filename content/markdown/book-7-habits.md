#### Be Proactive

Choice lies between stimulus and response. Our behaviour is a function of our decisions, not our conditions. Do not blame outside causes for our behaviour. The correct response to mistakes is acknowledgement and learning.

#### Begin with the end in mind

It is possible to be busy without being effective. Our lives become very different once we know what is deeply important. Leadership is the first creation, not management. Choice of action is clearest and most effective when centred upon principles.

#### Put first things first

Strength lies in doing what must be done regardless of motivation (discipline). Manage a week of actions, not time. Prioritise.

#### Think Win-Win

The third alternative—not my way or your way, but a better way. Develop an abundance mentality. Define end goals by first understanding win conditions for others.

#### Seek first to understand, then to be understood

Practice empathic listening. Sell solutions, not products. Build an emotional bank account.

#### Synergize

The world is greater than the sum of its parts. Collaborate. Raise difficulty, reduce competition. Perfect your strengths and compensate for your weaknesses.

#### Sharpen the Saw

Care for the four elements: physical, social, mental, and spiritual. Practice daily private victories. Sharpen skills weekly.
